Boiled eggs and toasts

This is a simple recipe. Perfect for a quick early breakfast before doing your workout session.

Consists in boiled eggs and toasted bread

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Water
  • Bread

Recipe Steps

  1. Put the eggs in a metal recipient filled with water
  2. Let the water boil
  3. Once the water starts boiling, wait for 6 minutes
  4. Take the eggs out, and put the bread on the toaster
  5. Peel the eggs
  6. Done!
