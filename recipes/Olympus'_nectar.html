Olympus' nectar

Olympus' nectar is a dish i invented while experimenting on something.

It consists in yogurt with a mix of different nuts and fruits

Ingredients

  • Flavorless Yogurt
  • Honey
  • Any variety of fruit (literally anything, you can throw in an avocado and it'll work out somehow)
  • Pumpkin seeds
  • Flax seeds

Recipe Steps

  1. Chop the fruit into tiny bits, the tinier the better
  2. Put it in a bowl
  3. Add the seeds
  4. Pour the yogurt
  5. Lastly, add the honey to sweeten the yogurt
  6. Done!
