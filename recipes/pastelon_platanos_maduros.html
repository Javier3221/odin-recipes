Pastelon de Platanos Maduros

This dish is called "pastelon de platanos maduros" ripe plaintain casserole in english

It consists in mashed plaintain, filled with meat (generally beef) and a coating of cheese

Ingredients

  • Plaintains
  • Meat of choice (If not sure, just use ground beef)
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Butter
  • Milk cream
  • Salt (amount depends on preference)

Recipe steps

  1. Cook the meat and boil the plaintains separately
  2. When the plaintains are well cooked, mash them with butter and milk cream
  3. In an oven plate, add a layer of plaintain, meat, plaintains and cheese
  4. Cook in oven
  5. Done!
